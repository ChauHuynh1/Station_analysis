The dataset consist of e-scooter rental information of 24 stations in a country. Description of each data field is as follow:

Station number
Date
Casual User: Number of non-registered customer who rent an e-scooter at the station in one day
Register User: Number of registered customer who rent an e-scooter at the station in one day
New registered user: Number of new registered user at the station in one day
Temperature: Average temperature at the station
Humidity: Average humidity at the station
Wind speed: Average wind speed at the station
Visibility: Average visibility at the station
Dew point temperature: Average dew point temperature at the station
Solar radiation: Average solar radiation at the station
Rainfall
Snowfall
Seasons
Opeartion Day: If the station is open
